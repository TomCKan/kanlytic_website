Data Analysis Services for Local Authorities

At Kanlytic, we provide clear and practical data analytics services designed to support evidence-based decision making in local government. Our goal is to help councils and public sector bodies turn complex data into meaningful insights that improve services, inform policy, and deliver better outcomes for communities.

Every authority faces unique challenges, from financial pressures to responding to evolving community needs. We adapt our proven approaches to align with your priorities, and where in-house data capacity is limited, we provide flexible support to ensure important projects are delivered effectively.

Get in touch for an initial consultation.


Some of the services we offer:

  • KPI Selection & Definition
    Work with stakeholders to identify the most relevant Key Performance Indicators or Evaluation measures for your authority. We help you select measures that genuinely reflect service delivery, community outcomes, and strategic objectives such as those in your corporate plan.

  • KPI Monitoring & Alerts
    Develop reliable systems to track and report on KPIs in real time. We design dashboards and automated alerts so officers and leadership teams can respond quickly to emerging risks and opportunities.

  • Data Cleaning
    Ensure your data is accurate, consistent, and ready for analysis. We manage duplicates, missing values, inconsistencies, and formatting issues, giving you a solid foundation for reporting, audits, and statutory returns.

  • Power BI Dashboard Development & Data Visualization
    Turn complex datasets into clear, interactive dashboards tailored to your service areas whether thatâ€™s housing, social care, or planning. We use tools like Power BI to design visual reports with full documentation, so your teams can maintain and update them independently.

  • Process Automation
    Reduce administrative burden and minimise errors by automating repetitive data tasks. We streamline data collection, reporting, and transformation using Python, SQL, and Excel macros, freeing staff to focus on higher-value work that directly benefits residents.

  • Demand & Budget Forecasting
    Forecast service demand and financial trends to support long-term planning and resource allocation. Using historical and regional data, we provide models that can be adjusted for local economic factors, enabling more accurate budgeting and medium-term financial strategies.
